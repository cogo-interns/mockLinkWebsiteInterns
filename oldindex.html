Link Internships

The Link Studios Summer Internship Program is a ten-week immersive experience designed for curious and driven students passionate about artificial intelligence (AI) and software development. The program begins with a four-week crash course in AI, machine learning, and modern web technologies, where interns gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge tools and culminate in a showcase event called “AI Day.” The following six weeks focus on collaborative, startup-style product development, as interns work in agile teams to design and prototype AI-driven solutions in high-value markets, culminating in a final demo day and business pitch. Ideal candidates are problem solvers and eager learners who thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments and are excited to tackle real-world challenges.
